# Life Hack or Urban Myth? 

An app made with Kotlin and Android Studio.
You read a statement and guess if it is a real life hack or a myth.

## What the App Does

- Shows a welcome screen with a Start button
- Asks you 5 questions one by one
- Tells you if your answer is right or wrong
- Shows your total score at the end
- Lets you review all the answers


## Files in the Project

- MainActivity.kt - the welcome screen
- FlashcardActivity.kt - shows the questions
- ScoreActivity.kt - shows your score
- ReviewActivity.kt - shows all the answers
